# Ingredients
- Onion
- Aubergine (400g)
- Zucchini (500g)
- Diced canned tomatoes (500g)
- Tomatoes (500g)
- Tomato paste
- Spices
	- Basilikum
	- Oregano
	- Rosmarin
	- Thymian
	- Kurkuma
	- Paprika
	- Fondor
	- Pepper
	- Salt

**Servings:** 7
# Preparation
1. Dice onion
2. Wash and grate Zucchini + Aubergine
3. Sear onion - add Zucchini + Aubergine - add tomato paste
   -> deglaze with tomato sauce
   -> simmer for min. 15min
4. Puree and add spice
