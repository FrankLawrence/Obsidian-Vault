# Ingredients
- 1kg chicken thighs
- 120ml soy sauce
- 60ml hoisin sauce
- 40ml Shaoxing
- 15ml sesame oil
- 9g sugar
- 4 garlic cloves
- 15g ginger
- 12g cornflower
- 2 bell peppers
- Spring onion
- (Can add gochujang)
- Rice
- Szechuan pepper

# Steps
Add sauces + garlic, ginger & szechuan pepper
Cut chicken
Pour some sauce over chicken and mix
Cut vegetables
Make the rice
Cook chicken
Remove chicken and add vegetables
Add back chicken
Add sauce
